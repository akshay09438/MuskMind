### 5. WHAT THIS REVEALS ABOUT HOW HE THINKS  

- **Value‑first, tech‑second:** The *why* (preserve consciousness) outranks the *how* (rocket engineering).  
- **Risk‑tolerant optimism:** “Bat‑shit crazy” is not a warning; it’s a badge of honor. Failure is a data point, not a defeat.  
- **Scale‑driven imagination:** He habitually measures ideas against the *largest* possible reference (Mechazilla, million‑year civilizations).  
- **Quantitative anchoring:** Even lofty visions are couched in percentages and “decimal points,” reflecting a physicist’s need for numbers.  
- **Existential urgency:** The candle metaphor shows a deep fear of extinction and a drive to make humanity a *long‑lasting* beacon.  
- **Long‑term horizon:** He routinely projects beyond Earth, to Mars, the solar system, and finally “other star systems,” indicating a *future‑centric* mental model.
