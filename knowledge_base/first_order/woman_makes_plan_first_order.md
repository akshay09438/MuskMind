### 1. The Core Life Philosophy – **Plan, Then Act, Even When the Map Is Blank**

> *“If you wait for certainty, you will wait forever. The only reliable compass is the act of planning itself.”*  

Maye Musk opens the book with the principle that has guided every chapter of her life: **the habit of making a concrete plan, however provisional, and then moving forward with decisive action.** She explains that a plan is not a promise of perfection; it is a *promise to yourself* that you will not be paralyzed by the unknown. The process is three‑fold:

1. **Clarify the Goal** – Write it down in a single sentence.  
2. **Chunk the Goal** – Break it into 30‑day, 90‑day, and 1‑year milestones.  
3. **Commit to the First Step** – Even a tiny, measurable action (e.g., “research three modeling agencies” or “schedule a blood‑work appointment”) is enough to create momentum.

She repeats the mantra throughout the book: *Plan → Act → Adjust → Repeat.* The “adjust” step acknowledges that uncertainty will always be present, but it also reinforces that the plan itself is a living document, not a static contract.

### 5. Health & Nutrition Wisdom – From Dietitian to Model

Maye’s nutrition philosophy is distilled into **four pillars** that she calls the **“Musk Method”:**

1. **Whole‑Food First** – Prioritize unprocessed foods; a plate should be 70 % vegetables, fruits, legumes, and whole grains.  
2. **Protein Precision** – Aim for 0.8 g of high‑quality protein per kilogram of body weight, adjusted for activity level.  
3. **Hydration Discipline** – Drink half your body weight (in ounces) of water daily; add electrolytes when exercising >30 minutes.  
4. **Mindful Eating** – Eat without screens, chew each bite 20–30 times, and pause for a breath before each new mouthful.

She interweaves personal anecdotes: the *“Blueberry‑Banana Power Smoothie”* that kept her energy stable during marathon modeling shoots, and the *“South African Rooibos Tea”* ritual that helped her manage cortisol spikes during high‑stress negotiations.
