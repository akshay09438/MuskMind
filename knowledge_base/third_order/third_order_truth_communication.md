### 1. THE CORE BELIEF  
Elon Musk holds that *truth is the only low‑entropy substrate that lets a complex engineering system iterate at exponential speed*.  A statement that matches reality becomes an external ledger, eliminating the hidden “mental‑accounting” work required to keep a lie coherent.  Because every lie injects informational entropy, it forces the organization into a slower, serial mode where trust— the low‑entropy resource that enables parallel teams, regulators, and customers— is depleted.  Consequently, any communication strategy that tolerates white lies, ideological “idea pathogens,” or narrative capture is a strategic liability; the only defensible stance is to broadcast facts (or admitted uncertainty) even when uncomfortable, and to treat speech as a utility whose open‑air bandwidth must be maximized rather than throttled.
